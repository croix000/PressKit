Description

Summon a powerful army of feline minions to do your bidding in this tactical, roguelike card-battler. Explore randomly generated dungeons and build powerful combos to fight off hordes of evil. Can you survive these challenges and become the ultimate Nekomancer?

History

Play as a clever cat mage with the brilliant ability to summon other cats to your side. Use your skills in feline manipulation to battle enemy cat summoners, known as "Nekomancers". Taking place in world where humans are able to use "Nekos" for their own benefit, as the only sentient cat alive it's up to you to uncover the mysterious origins of your kind in this colourful, cat-filled fantasy world.

Features

  • As you progress through the game, you'll have the opportunity to build your own deck of cards, each with unique abilities and powers. Choose your cards carefully to create a balanced and effective strategy.
  • In Nekomancer, each dungeon run is different, with randomly generated levels and challenges. This means that you'll need to adapt to each new situation and use your skills to survive.
  • In addition to your deck of cards, you'll also be able to summon a variety of feline companions to fight by your side. Each cat has its own unique abilities, and you can upgrade them as you progress through the game.
  • As you explore the dungeon, you'll encounter powerful boss monsters that will test your skills and strategies. Can you defeat these formidable foes and emerge victorious?
